About Us

Sangeeta Asri Academy is a cultural institution dedicated to nurturing talent in Indian classical music and dance. Founded by renowned artist Guru Smt. Sangeeta Asri, the academy emphasizes tradition, discipline, and excellence.

Our mission is to create an environment where students of all ages can immerse themselves in the beauty of the arts and develop not just skill, but a deep connection to Indian heritage.

With decades of performance and teaching experience, the academy offers personalized instruction in Hindustani vocals, Kathak dance, and instrumental music.
